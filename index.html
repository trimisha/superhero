Characters

Tony Stark

Is a billionaire genius and inventor who built the Iron Man suit to escape captivity. With his sharp wit, incredible tech, and charm, he quickly became the heart and soul of the Avengers—bringing brains, humor, and a whole lot of sass to the team.

Thor

Thor is the God of Thunder and heir to the throne of Asgard. He wields the mighty Mjolnir and commands the power of lightning. Oh, and let’s not forget he’s famous for his legendary hair and even more legendary battles.

Hulk

Bruce Banner's alter ego is the Hulk; when he gets angry, he transforms into a massive green powerhouse. And when it comes to showing affection, smashing is his way of saying 'I care.'

Captain America

Steve Rogers: the original super soldier. Shield in hand, justice in heart, and a timeless “I can do this all day” energy.

Black Widow

Natasha Romanoff: spy, assassin, Avenger. Deadly with her fists and sharp with her mind. Don’t underestimate the Widow.
